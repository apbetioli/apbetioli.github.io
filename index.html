About

My first program was a quiz coded in a Windows 95 ".bat" file, created after long hours exploring files on my computer, trying to understand how programs worked, and using a BASIC programming language book as a resource. When I discovered the internet, I began researching how to create my own video game, which led me into programming a FPS game (First Person Shooter), where the gun was a fish that shot fireworks. I found my passion in programming.

I have over 15 years of experience in software development, working with various technologies, projects, and teams. I also ventured into running my own company, fulfilling the roles of content creator, video editor, marketing specialist, and software engineer.

For the past two years, I've been working remotely for an AI company based in Berlin, creating tools for AI developers in the NLP field.

I am passionate about building things, independent of the tech stack. I also enjoy mentoring new engineers and learn with them.
